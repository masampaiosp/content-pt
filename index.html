▶ Important Information for Code

Important

User Intent

Sports bettors search for promotional codes to maximize their chances and receive extra benefits on betting sites. They want to:

  • Find valid codes that offer advantages like free bets, welcome bonuses, or deposit multipliers
  • Learn step-by-step how to activate these codes to avoid missing out on offers properly
  • Know in detail all rules and requirements needed to qualify for bonuses, including minimum deposit amounts and validity periods
  • Discover which sports and types of bets the codes can be used on, ensuring they can take advantage of the benefit in their preferred categories

Tone and Voice

  • Use active and direct language
  • Maintain an informative tone without promotional exaggeration
  • Prioritize clarity and objectivity in explanations
  • We are not the bookmaker, so don’t use the pronoun “us” (or “our bonuses”, “our promos,” etc.)

Keywords

Remember: If you’re using Surfer SEO, Yoast, or any tool that checks your content, don’t take recommendations literally. This can take the natural tone of the article. We must always think about user intent first.

  • Keyword Stuffing: We do not recommend that practice. Place the main keyword at the top of the article and naturally 2 or 3 times. You can use the H2 also.
  • Keywords that are misspelled should always be corrected, regardless of what the brief says;
  • The same applies to accents;
  • Capitalization must also be corrected since they affect the overall quality of the text and may harm compliance;
  • Hyphens, dots, and spaces should be appropriately used as well, since they do not affect ranking, but harm the quality of the text if incorrect;

The user's intent when searching for bonuses at betting sites is primarily to maximize their initial or ongoing betting value and returns.
